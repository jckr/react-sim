`roundRectangle` takes an object as argument with the following properties:

All the [CanvasFrame](/api/#canvasframe) properties, plus:

|Name|Description|
|----|-----------|
|__x__<br/>number|x coordinate of top-left corner of rectangle|
|__y__<br/>number|y coordinate of top-left corner of rectangle|
|__width__<br/>number|width of rectangle|
|__height__<br/>number|height of rectangle|
|__tl__<br/>number, optional|radius of top-left corner|
|__tr__<br/>number, optional|radius of top-right corner|
|__bl__<br/>number, optional|radius of bottom-left corner|
|__br__<br/>number, optional|radius of botrom-right corner|
|__r__<br/>number, optional|radius of all unspecified corners|
